Art of Mine Accessories JA, a small female-owned business, commenced its journey on January 5, 2021. Specializing in crafting unique items from resin, acrylic, and wood, including keychains, clocks, signs, paintings, and decor, Art of Mine partnered with 1930 Media to launch on social media.

Our mission was clear: foster a vibrant community around the brand and drive sales. We set up and actively managed social media profiles, posted consistently, and ran targeted ads, amassing 200 followers in the first month. To boost engagement, we initiated an exciting five-day giveaway that encouraged followers to repost their favorite pieces and tag friends. This effort resulted in a 25% growth in followers and engagement.

Since then, Art of Mine has flourished, now boasting a dedicated following of 652 supporters. Furthermore, they've successfully launched a website where customers can conveniently purchase ready-made items and place custom orders to suit their preferences.

Notably, the Jamaica Constabulary Force (JCF) discovered Art of Mine, leading to the creation of custom clocks and pieces. This collaboration highlights our commitment to nurturing emerging businesses into thriving success stories.
